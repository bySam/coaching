Coaching

Sam | @400apm

My coaching focuses on the fundamentals of mechanics that aren't strictly mouse control. Primarily: strafeaim, positioning, dodge, aim strategy, psychology, and how they all relate to each other.

To get coaching, join the d0dgerz discord and DM me there. Before getting coaching please read my Fundamentals of Strafe guide, so that you're at least somewhat familiar with the material. It's okay if you don't understand all of it completely, we'll discuss it during the session, but read it at least once.

Required: read my Fundamentals of Strafe guide.
